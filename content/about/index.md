Growing up listening to the Saturday morning kid's programming on the radio, Josh has always loved audio drama. After going to college for radio production, Josh worked for Moody Radio on multiple nationally syndicated radio programs and helped start Moody Radio's first podcast-only program.

Josh later moved to Crossway where he helped start the audio production department and worked on over 200 audiobooks, 8 audio Bibles, and a variety of podcasts.
